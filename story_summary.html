The One Thread Connecting All Three Videos

The number on the brochure is always a lie. The gold price hides the rupee depreciation story. The CTC hides the in-hand reality. The home price hides the interest, the taxes, the GST, the corpus fund, the interiors.

The media manufactures FOMO at every level. "Gold is up 13% — buy now." "Your friends have 1Cr CTC — you're behind." "Everyone's buying in Bangalore — don't miss out." The data never supports the panic.

The formula that actually works across all three topics is identical: know the full cost, not the headline number. Keep your fixed commitments low. Save a large fraction of what you earn. Diversify across assets. Don't make irreversible decisions under FOMO pressure.
